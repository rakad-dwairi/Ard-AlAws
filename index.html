Why Use LPG?

LPG is less costly, cleaner, safer, and more environmentally friendly and the most versatile energy source as compared to others.

Its unique composition and versatility means it can be used in virtually every location.

As a testament to the efficiency of LPG, it is 1.6 % of Europe’s total energy consumption, decreasing Europe’s energy dependency. LPG will help Europe achieve its state goal of 20% increase in energy efficiency by 2020. Over 150,000 people are employed in this sector, and it meets the energy demands of more than 20 + million European citizens (est. 2009).

Corporate Social Investment

“ At Ardh Al-Aws Co. “

we provide LPG distribution and pipeline services by investing in those people that are in distant need. We understand that by giving back to these communities, we are able to contribute our part to areas that are in need

We believe that people are entitled to have equal access to recourses of this nature. We strive to provide LPG energy to those living in more remote parts of the region.

A pipeline network servicing remote areas (private residencies, etc.) can be facilitated by a single storage facility and a subterranean system of pipes delivering LPG to these hard to reach areas. When implementing the delivery of LPG to areas of this nature, we take all the necessary steps to ensure minimal disturbances to both the landscape and population benefitting from LPG access. Effective in comparison to other energy sources, a ‘meter’ can be fitted to ensure clients are charged only for what they use ensuring there it is a competitive service with no hidden costs.
